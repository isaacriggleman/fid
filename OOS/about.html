About Orlando Orangutan Sanctuary

The Orlando Orangutan Sanctuary is all about creating a safe home for orangutans who cannot return to the wild. We here at Orlando Orangutan Sanctuary (OOS) pride ourselves on the environment we have created for these beautiful forrest creatures. We accept donations which go to feeding and maintaining our beatiful sanctuary here in Orlando. You can also reserve time slots here at OOS to get a chance to interact with these beautiful primates. All funds from private visitations go to maintaining our sanctuary.

If you'd like to schedule a visitation, feel free to email us at OrlandoOS@gmail.com.
