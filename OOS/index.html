The Sanctuary

The Orlando Orangutan Sanctuary offers a safe and relaxed atmosphere for orangutauns who cannot return to the wild. All of the orangutans here are perminent residents and can be visited by setting up an appoitment with our team!.

Location and Hours

Orlando, Florida; Visits can be schedueled Monday through Thursday 8am to 3pm; Friday and Saturday, 10am to 5pm!

Our Family Motto

We vow to provide every one of our many orangutan residents with not only a safe place to live but give them the enviorment they need to fully flourish!.
